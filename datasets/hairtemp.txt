Dataset:  hairtemp.dat

Source: P. Eaton, M.W. Eaton (1937). "Temperature and the Growth
of Hair", Science, Vol. 86, #2233, p. 354

Description: Facial Hair growth and daily temperature measurements
(averages of 10 measurements per month)

Variables/Columns
Month  7-8
Average Temp   15-16  /* Degrees F  */
Facial Hair Growth  22-24  /* mmx1000  */
